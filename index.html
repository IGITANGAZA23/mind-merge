How to Apply

Start your journey to study in South Korea with these steps:

  • Choose your program and university
  • Check admission requirements (GPA, language proficiency)
  • Prepare documents (transcripts, recommendation letters)
  • Apply through university portals or Study in Korea website
  • Apply for a student visa (D-2) upon acceptance
